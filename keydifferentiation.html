Key Differentiation

Since our inception, i-Magination's software and solutions have been well received by our clients across the Asia Pacific, including Australia, Hong Kong, China, Korea, Singapore, Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand, Indonesia and Brunei. These clients appreciate the values we offer such as:

1. Comprehensive Features i-Magination's software and solution offer comprehensive Features that cover the complete document life cycle from capture, manage, deliver to distribute and provides multi-channels input and output delivery channels to facilitate information and knowledge sharing both within and outside of the enterprise. Our flagship product, the Document WorkBench® is an integrated, yet modular suite of software.

2. High Security i-Magination's emphasis in security has earned Document WorkBench's recognition as one of the most secured Document Management software in the world. It enables the enterprise to safeguard its document from unauthorised access and usage, by providing 250 levels of classification.

3. Strong Domain i-Magination's Research and Development staff and Solution consultants are highly experienced professionals focused in providing ECM and BPM software and solution. Our focus has enabled the company to attract and build a wealth of strong domain experiences over the years.

4. Competitive Price i-Magination's strategy in promoting in-house developed software and continuous R&D enable us to market and implement our software and solution at a competitive cost. This allows our customers to enjoy faster return from their investment.

5. Proven Quality i-Magination's project management and development methodologies from analysis, design, development, implementation through to post support are based on ISO 9001:2000 Quality Management Standard. Our stringent quality ensures that our customers enjoy their peace of mind with our deliverables.
